Hello

I am Doğukan Yıldız. I was born in Ardahan in 1997, and I have approached gaining experience in different fields with great passion throughout my life. I would like to share with you a few important stops on my educational and career journey.

I completed my undergraduate education in the Department of Physical Education and Sports Teaching at Ardahan University. At the same time, I strengthened my interest in computers and technology with software training I received earlier.

For many years, I worked on computer hardware at Aydın Bilgisayar in Mersin. This process took place during my youth, between the ages of 15-19, and laid the foundation for my journey into the world of computer technology.

In 2022, I stepped into the software industry and worked as a Front-End Developer at Ankara-Neos Software Academy for 6 months. During this period, I got acquainted with modern web technologies, developed user-friendly interfaces, and actively participated in projects. These experiences greatly contributed to improving my skills in the software world.

In 2023, in order to further develop myself and play a more effective role in the world of technology, I started the Computer Programming department. My curiosity about computer programming and my tendency to research constantly contribute significantly to my rise in the software industry.
